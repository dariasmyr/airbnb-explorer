Welcome to the Airbnb Explorer! This page shows the results of our data analysis on Airbnb listings in New York City.

Overview

The Airbnb Explorer project aims to provide users with insights into Airbnb listings in New York City. By analyzing data on Airbnb listings, this project helps users find the perfect rental for their needs.

The price of a rental is one of the most important factors when choosing a rental. The price of a rental is determined by the number of bedrooms, the location, and the amenities. The chart below shows the mean price per neighbourhood. The mean price per neighbourhood is calculated by taking the mean price of all rentals in a neighbourhood.
